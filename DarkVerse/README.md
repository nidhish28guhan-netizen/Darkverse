## 🌌 Overview

DarkVerse is a **frontend-only** interactive companion built for fans of **Netflix's DARK**. Instead of browsing traditional wiki pages, explore the story through dynamic family trees, timelines, relationships, mysteries, locations, organizations, and knowledge graphs—all while staying spoiler-safe based on your selected season and episode.

## 🎯 Vision

DarkVerse recreates the experience of discovering **DARK** as you watch it.

Every time you change the **Season** or **Episode**, the entire application updates to reveal only the information available up to that point—keeping every twist and reveal spoiler-free.
